Not sure what mentoring is all about and where to start?




Why should i mentor?



It is immensely gratifying and powerful to mentor others. You can significantly influence people's careers and are almost certainly going to learn something new in the process.


What does a mentor do ?



Simply explained, mentoring is the act of imparting your knowledge, expertise, and/or experience to another person in order to aid in their professional advancement.
