We support organizations in starting successful internal and external mentoring programs and in assisting individuals in finding mentors and becoming mentors.









How it works



For individuals:



The largest open mentorship platform that is free has been created by us. Anyone can register as a mentor, mentee, or both on Uncommon Mentorship for free.Once you've registered, we'll use our mentor matching system to find the best mentoring pairs for you.



For Organisations:



Your mentorship program, or programs, should be set up. Yes, you can easily run several programs in parallel. With your committed account support from Uncommon Mentorship, you are backed throughout. Create your own branding and logos, and customise your registration questions, matching algorithm, and approval procedures.



Invite your participants to join you after that. Upload links to your staff, students, membership communities, and pretty much any other participants you desire, along with CSV and Excel® Spreadsheets.

Become a mentor




The mentor serves as a role model, providing the mentee with insights into their own experiences and offering guidance on how to navigate complex situations.A mentor needs to embody qualities that include experience, empathy, patience positive attitude and trustworthiness.These qualities are essential for a mentor to be effective in their role and to help their mentee achieve their full potential.
